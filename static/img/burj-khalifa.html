  • Did you know?

    1

    Did you know?

    7 world records
    Other than the world's tallest free-standing structure, the Burj Khalifa has previously held the world records for longest elevator travel distance and highest restaurant in the world.

  • Did you know?

    2

    Did you know?

    100,000 elephants
    That's the approximate weight of the total amount of concrete used in the construction of the Burj Khalifa.

  • Did you know?

    3

    Did you know?

    26,000 hand-cut glass panels 
    The exterior facade is covered in 26,000 hand-cut glass panels, and it takes three months to clean the entire building!
